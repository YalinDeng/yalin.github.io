Introduction

I am a 2nd year PhD student at Brown University School of Public Health. Before entering the PhD program, I graduated from UT Austin with my PharmD and UC San Diego with a Bacholar of Science in Physiology and Neuroscience, and a Bacholar of Arts in Linguistics.

My research interests span the fields of pharmacoepidemiology, pharmacoeconomics, and health services research.
