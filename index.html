Introduction

I am a 4th year pharmacy student at The University of Texas at Austin College of Pharmacy. I am currently on rotation with Scott and White Health Plan. Before entering pharmacy school, I graduated from UC San Diego with a Bacholar of Science in Physiology and Neuroscience, and a Bacholar of Arts in Linguistics.

My research interests span in pharmacoepidemiology and health services research.
